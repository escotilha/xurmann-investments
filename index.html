01 Philosophy
"We believe in patient capital deployed behind exceptional operators building enduring businesses in underserved markets."

Xurmann Investments serves as a private holding company focused on long-term value creation through strategic investments in technology-enabled businesses, with particular emphasis on Latin American markets where structural inefficiencies create compelling opportunities.

Our approach combines rigorous financial analysis with deep operational understanding, allowing us to identify and support businesses positioned for sustainable growth. We favor companies with strong unit economics, defensible market positions, and management teams aligned for the long term.

Rather than seeking quick exits, we build lasting partnerships with founders and operators who share our commitment to disciplined growth and value creation.

02 Focus Areas
01

B2B Software

Enterprise and SMB software businesses with recurring revenue models, strong retention metrics, and clear paths to profitability. Particular interest in vertical SaaS serving specialized industries.

02

Latin American Markets

Technology and technology-enabled businesses serving Brazilian and broader Latin American markets, where growing digital adoption creates significant opportunities for well-positioned operators.

03

Strategic Acquisitions

Platform and add-on acquisitions that create value through operational improvement, market consolidation, or strategic repositioning. Focus on businesses with strong fundamentals and clear value creation levers.

03 Investment Principles
01

Permanence Over Exit

We invest with the intention to hold indefinitely. Our holding period is "forever" unless fundamentals deteriorate or significantly better capital allocation opportunities emerge.

02

Alignment of Interests

We partner with management teams who have meaningful ownership stakes and whose incentives are aligned with long-term value creation rather than short-term metrics.

03

Margin of Safety

Disciplined capital deployment requires acquiring assets at valuations that provide adequate downside protection while maintaining exposure to upside potential.

04

Operational Excellence

Beyond capital, we contribute strategic guidance and operational expertise to help portfolio companies accelerate growth and improve execution.
